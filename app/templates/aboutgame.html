About the Game

This game specificallty targets college graduates, but may help other people outside that catogory, that fear the real world. We want people to be comfortable learning how the stock market works and how they affect the enviornment unintentionally. This simulator allows them to buy stocks, shares, and eco-friendly products within their fix balance of $10,000. We want everyone to see how their action have consequences, in both financial and environmental aspects. The player's name is Kaiden, it is a Irish name for battler. In this game you will require the player to battle money wisely. Let's see if you live up to this name.
