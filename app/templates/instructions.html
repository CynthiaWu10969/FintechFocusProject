How to play

The Stock and Climate Change Simulator

.

Instructions

This is a survival game. You will have to follow a few steps to live through the rounds.

1) A player will start off with a balance of $10,000 and a health index of 100. They would need to select any stock they would want to buy, they may choose any stock outside the set examples.

2) The player would have to pick a date from the calendar to determine what day they would like to buy the stock. However, it has to be any friday of any month before today.

3) The page will automatically bring you to the next step which is to choose how many shares they would want to buy. They can buy the exact number of shares they can afford (which will be noted on the top of the screen and on the bottom right), but it is adviced you buy less than that. The player will need money left over to buy eco-friendly products to survive the next round.

4) The next round is to buy eco- friendly products to save the environment. There are mutliple selections to choose from. The player may choose up to two products to buy. Based on the players decisions they can see how far they have came alone, their balance and health levels.
